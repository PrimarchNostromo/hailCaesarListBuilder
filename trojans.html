Mycenaean Trojans

These are the rules for making a Mycenaean Trojan army

Infantry 75% At least 3/4 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Medium infantry 25% of infantry At least 1/4 of the non-skirmisher infantry units in the army must be Trojan medium infantry units of one kind or another
Chariots up to 25% Up to 1/4 of the units in the army can be chariots
Divisions 4+ units unless cavalry Divisions must contain at least 4 units and must be led by a commander..
Skirmishers per division 50% of infantry Divisions may contain up to one half as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
