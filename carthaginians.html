Carthaginian List Builder

These are the rules for making a Carthaginian army

Infantry 66% At least 2/3 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Libyan spearmen up to 25% of infantry Up to 1/4 of the non-skirmishing infantry can be Libyan spearmen
Cavalry up to 25% Up to 1/4 of the units in the army can be cavalry
Light cavalry at least 50% of calvary At least 1/2 of the cavalry must be light cavalry
Elephants up to 10% Up to 1/10 units in the army can be elephants
Divisions 4+ units Divisions must contain at least 4 units excluding skirmishers and must be led by a commander.
Skirmishers per division 50% of infantry Divisions may contain up to half as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
