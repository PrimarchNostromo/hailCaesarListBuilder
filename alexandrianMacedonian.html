Alexandrian Macedonians

These are the rules for making a Alexandrian Macedonian army

Infantry 50% At least half of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Cavalry up to 50% Up to half of the units in the army can be cavalry
Pike 75%+ of infantry At least three quarters of the non-skirmisher infantry units in the army must comprise pike-armed phalanx of one kind or another
Artillery 1 of each type up to 10% Up to one unit of each type and no more than one in 10 of the number of units in the army in total
Divisions 4+ units unless cavalry Divisions must contain at least 4 units (except cavalry divisions) and must be led by a commander. If the Hetairoi Companion guard are fielded they must form part of the general's division
Skirmishers per division 50% of infantry Divisions may contain up to one quarter as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
