These are the rules for making a Republican Roman army

Infantry 75% At least 3/4 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Hastati, Principes, Triarii 50% infantry Together, Hastati, Pricipes, and Triarii must make up 50% of non-skirmish infantry. Hastati and principes units must be bought in pairs. Triarii can be inlcuded in the ratio of 1 unit of triarii for every 2 pairs of hastati/principes
Cavalry up to 25% Up to 1/4 of the units in the army can be cavalry
Light cavalry at least 50% of calvary At least 1/2 of the cavalry must be light cavalry
Divisions 4+ units Divisions must contain at least 4 units excluding skirmishers and must be led by a commander. Allied infantry must be fielded in divisions wholly composed of the same native troops
Skirmishers per division 50% of infantry Divisions may contain up to half as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
