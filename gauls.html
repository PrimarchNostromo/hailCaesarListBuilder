These are the rules for making a Gallic army

Infantry 75% At least 3/4 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Warbands 50% of infantry At least half the non-skirmisher infantry units in the army must be medium infantry warbands of one kind or other.
Cavalry up to 25% Up to 1/4 of the units in the army can be cavalry
Chariots up to 10% if no medium cavalry Up to one in ten of the army can be chariots so long as the army contains no medium cavalry
Divisions 4+ units Divisions must contain at least 4 units excluding skirmishers and must be led by a commander. If general's guard is fielded these must form part of the general's division. If German allies are included they must form a division together with any skirmishers as noted below
Skirmishers per division 50% of infantry Divisions may contain up to half as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
