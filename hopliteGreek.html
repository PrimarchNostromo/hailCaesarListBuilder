Hoplite Greek

These are the rules for making a Hoplite Greek army

Infantry 75% At least 3/4 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Hoplites 75% of infantry At least three quarters of the non-skirmisher infantry units in the army must comprise hoplites of one kind or another
Spartans up to 50% of hoplites Up to half of hoplite units can be spartans
Cavalry up to 10% Up to 1/10 of the units in the army can be cavalry
Divisions 4+ units unless cavalry Divisions must contain at least 4 units (except cavalry divisions) and must be led by a commander. A division containing entirely of cavalry can be any size so long as it includes all the calvary in the army.
Skirmishers per division 25% of infantry Divisions may contain up to one quarter as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry

Commander
Command must be exercised in person to be effective.
 1 commander must be provided per division. All commanders including general have leadership 8.
Upgrade division commander to General 1 commander must be provided per division. Your army must have one General
Upgrade commander to a Spartan General with leadership 9
"Come back with this shield, or on it..."
