Sung China List Builder

These are the rules for making a Sung Chinese army:

Infantry 50% At least 1/2 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Medium/heavy infantry in mixed ranks 50%+ of infantry At least half of the non-skirmisher infantry units in the army must be mixed medium or heavy infantry
Cavalry up to 25% At least 1/4 of the units in the army can be cavalry
Heavy/medium cavalry at least 50% of calvary At least 1/2 of the cavalry must be either heavy or medium cavalry
Artillery - up to 25% Up to 1/4 of the units in the army can be artillery
Divisions 4+ units Divisions must contain at least 4 units excluding skirmishers and must be led by a commander.
Skirmishers per division 25% of infantry Divisions may contain up to a quarter as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
