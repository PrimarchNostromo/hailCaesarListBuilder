Plantagenet English List Builder

These are the rules for making a Plantagenet English army:

Infantry 25% At least 1/4 of the units must be non-skirmishing infantry
Feudal/Mercenary non-missile infantry 25% of infantry At least a quarter of the non-skirmishing units in the army must be non-missile armed feudal heavy infantry, mercenary heavy infantry, or mercenary medium infantry
Bows 50% of missile armed infantry if present If bow-armed medium or light infantry are included they must make up at least half of all missile-armed medium or light infantry units
Cavalry 25%+ At least 1/4 of the units in the army must be cavalry
Knights at least 50% of calvary At least 1/2 of the cavalry must be knights
Divisions 4+ units Divisions must contain at least 4 units excluding skirmishers and must be led by a commander.
Skirmishers per division 25% of infantry Divisions may contain up to a quarter as many skirmisher units as they contain non-skirmisher infantry
